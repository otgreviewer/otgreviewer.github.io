Week 1 - Arithmetic Sequence, Arithmetic Series, Geometric Sequence

Arithmetic Sequence (Equation) $$a_n=a_1+(n-1)~d$$
$$a_1:~is~the ~first~ term$$ $$n: ~is ~the ~number ~of ~terms$$ $$1: ~is~ just~ constant$$ $$d: ~is ~the ~common ~difference$$
Arithmetic Series (Equation) $$S_n=\frac{n}{2}(a_1 + a_n)~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~(1)$$ $$S_n=\frac{n}{2}(2a_1+(n-1)~d)~~~~~~~(2)$$
$$a_n: is ~the~ n^{th} ~term$$
Geometric Sequence (Equation) $$a_n=a_1~r^{n-1}$$
$$a_n: n^{th}~term~of~the~sequence$$ $$a_1: first~term~of~the~sequence$$ $$r: common~ratio$$
